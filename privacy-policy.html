1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Website Audit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you request a full audit report, we collect your email address to send you the report and store it in our database for future purposes (with your consent).
  • Website URL: We collect the website URL you submit for auditing purposes.
  • Audit Results: We store the audit results associated with your email address and website URL.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to remember your cookie preferences and improve your experience.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our service, such as timestamps and IP addresses (anonymized).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our AI website audit service
  • To send you the audit report via email
  • To store your information in our database for future purposes (with your explicit consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To analyze and improve our service quality

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: You have given explicit consent for us to process your email address and store it in our database
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to provide you with the audit report you requested
  • Legitimate Interests: We have a legitimate interest in improving our service and analyzing usage patterns

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 10.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Our cookie usage includes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (cookie consent preferences)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (only with your consent)

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the cookie settings in your browser or through our cookie consent banner.

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as email service providers) to deliver our services. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and GDPR compliance requirements.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights under GDPR, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ai-website-audit.com
  • Subject Line: "GDPR Data Request" or "Privacy Inquiry"

We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by GDPR.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
